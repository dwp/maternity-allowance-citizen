Apply for Maternity Allowance

Maternity Allowance is a payment you can get when you take time off to have a baby.

You could get it if you:

  • are employed but cannot get Statutory Maternity Pay (SMP)
  • are self-employed
  • have recently stopped working
  • take part in unpaid work for the business of your spouse or civil partner

You can get Maternity Allowance for up to 39 weeks.

Start now

Before you start

You will need to know your National Insurance number.

You will also need to upload scans of your original Maternity Certificate (MAT B1) and an SMP1 form from each employer that you had during the 15th week before the week in which your baby is due.
