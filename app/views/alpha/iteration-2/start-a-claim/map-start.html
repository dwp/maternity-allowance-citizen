When do you want your Maternity Allowance Payments to start?

It can take up to 8 weeks to register a Maternity Allowance claim if there are no mistakes in the application.If you are still working on or after the 11th week before the week the baby is due, your Maternity Allowance Period or 14-week period can start on any day after you stop work, but no later than the day after the baby is born.

If you have stopped working, your Maternity Allowance Period or 14-week period will start on the Sunday of the 11th week before the week the baby is due.
