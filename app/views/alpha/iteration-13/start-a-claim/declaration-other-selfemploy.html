Effects on other benefits - self-employed

The other benefits that can be affected are:

  • Bereavement benefits
  • Carer's Allowance
  • Carer support payment
  • Council tax reduction
  • Employment and Support Allowance (ESA)
  • Housing Benefit
  • Income Support

Jobseeker’s Allowance (JSA) will stop if you get Maternity Allowance.

You can use the benefit calculator (opens in new tab) to help what benefits you could get and how much you could receive.

Payments

Most benefits are paid every 2 or 4 weeks. If you’re getting any other benefits and Maternity Allowance, these may fall on different days.
