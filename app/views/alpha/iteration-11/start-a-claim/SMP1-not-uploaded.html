You need an SMP1 form to get Maternity Allowance

If you have more than 1 employer, you must send us an SMP1 form from each employer.

If your employer refuses to give you an SMP1 form, you can contact HM Revenue & Customs (HMRC) (opens in new tab) for help.

You can still continue with your application without your SMP1 form. We’ll contact you later and you’ll need to send it to us through post.
