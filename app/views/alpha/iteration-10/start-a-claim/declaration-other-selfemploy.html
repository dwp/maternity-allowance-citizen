Effects on other benefits

The other benefits that can be affected are:

  • Bereavement benefits
  • Carer's Allowance
  • Carer support payment
  • Council tax reduction
  • Employment Support Allowance (ESA)
  • Housing Benefit
  • Income Support
  • Jobseeker's Allowance - this will stop if you get Maternity Allowance

You can use the benefit calculator to help what benefits you could get and how much you could receive.

Payments

Other benefits and Maternity Allowance could be paid at different times. Most benefits are paid monthly or every 4 weeks. Maternity Allowance is paid every 2 or 4 weeks.

Pregnancy-related sick leave

If you are receiving New Style Employment and Support Allowance or Incapcity Benefit in this 4 week period, it will stop.
