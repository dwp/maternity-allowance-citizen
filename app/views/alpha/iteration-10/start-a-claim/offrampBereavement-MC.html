You are not eligible for Maternity Allowance

As your baby died and was born before 24 weeks of pregnancy, you cannot apply for Maternity Allowance.

If your baby died, but was born after 24 weeks of pregnancy, or died after being born, then you can apply for Maternity Allowance.

After a miscarriage

A miscarriage can be emotionally and physically draining. If you are struggling, advice and support are available for you.

Support resources

  • NHS: After a miscarriage
  • Tommy’s: Support after miscarriage
  • Sands: Support for you

Baby loss certificate

You can get a free certificate in memory of your baby if they died before 24 weeks.

You do not have to request a certificate, and it’s not a legal document, but a way to recognise your experience.
