You are not eligible for Funeral Expenses Payment if you had a miscarriage

As your baby died and was born before 24 weeks of pregnancy, you cannot apply for Funeral Expenses Payment.

If your baby died, but was born after 24 weeks of pregnancy, or died after being born, then you can apply for Funeral Expenses Payment.

After a miscarriage

A miscarriage can be emotionally and physically draining. If you are struggling, advice and support are available for you.

Support resources

  • NHS: After miscarriage (opens in new tab)
  • Tommy’s: Support after miscarriage (opens in new tab)
  • Sands: Support for you (opens in new tab)

Baby loss certificate

You can get a free certificate in memory of your baby (opens in new tab) if they died before 24 weeks.

You do not have to request a certificate, and it’s not a legal document, but a way to remember your baby.
