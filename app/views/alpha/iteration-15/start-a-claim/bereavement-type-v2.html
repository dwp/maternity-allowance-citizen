Applying for Maternity Allowance after a bereavement

You can apply for Maternity Allowance if your baby dies or was stillborn within certain timeframes.

Our eligibility criteria is defined as:

Stillbirth

Your baby died, and then was born after 24 weeks of pregnancy and you have a stillbirth certificate


Neonatal death

Your baby was born alive at any stage of pregnancy and then died


Miscarriage

Your baby died and was born before 24 weeks of pregnancy
