How much you could get

You can get between £27 to £187.18 per week for up to 39 weeks if you’re registered as self-employed.

How much you get depends on how many Class 2 National Insurance contributions you’ve made in your Test Period (the 66 weeks before the week your baby is due.)

We contact HMRC on your behalf to find out how many contributions you’ve made.

If you have not paid enough contributions to get the full amount of Maternity Allowance, HMRC will invite you to top up your contributions to get the full amount.

You do not need to tell us if you choose to top up contributions, HMRC will do this for you.

You might get £27 for a few weeks while we link your contributions to your Maternity Allowance application. We will increase and backdate your payments, which may take several weeks.
